Why Choose Bangladesh for Studying MBBS Abroad?


With the increasing competition for medical seats in India, more and more students are opting to study MBBS abroad. Among the various destinations, Bangladesh stands out as a highly favorable option for Indian students. In recent years, Bangladesh has gained significant recognition for offering quality medical education at affordable costs. If you are an aspiring doctor and looking for an alternative to studying in India, Bangladesh offers numerous advantages that make it one of the top choices for pursuing MBBS abroad.


In this article, we will explore the benefits of studying MBBS in Bangladesh, including why it is a great choice for Indian students, the admission process, the cost structure, and how MBBS abroad consultants can assist you in navigating the entire journey.


  1. High-Quality Medical Education


    2. One of the primary reasons why Indian students choose Bangladesh for studying MBBS abroad is the quality of education. The medical colleges in Bangladesh are known for following the same curriculum as Indian medical colleges, and most of them are approved by the Medical Council of India (MCI). This ensures that the degree is valid in India, and students are eligible to sit for the Foreign Medical Graduates Examination (FMGE) to practice medicine in India.


    The teaching methodology focuses heavily on both theoretical knowledge and practical clinical training. With modern medical facilities and experienced faculty members, students are well-equipped to handle real-life medical situations, making them competent medical professionals.


  2. Affordable Fees and Living Costs


    3. One of the biggest concerns for Indian students seeking to study MBBS abroad is the cost involved. Compared to other popular countries like the USA, UK, or even Russia, Bangladesh offers a significantly more affordable MBBS program. The tuition fees in Bangladesh are considerably lower than in private medical colleges in India, making it a cost-effective option.


    The living expenses in Bangladesh are also very affordable. With similar lifestyles and cultural practices, Indian students can comfortably adapt to the environment without having to spend exorbitant amounts on food, accommodation, or travel. This cost-effectiveness is one of the key reasons why students choose to study MBBS in Bangladesh.


  3. MCI-Approved Universities


    4. When Indian students decide to study MBBS abroad, one of the most crucial factors they need to consider is whether the degree is recognized in India. Medical universities in Bangladesh are approved by the Medical Council of India (MCI), which ensures that the degree holders are eligible to practice in India after passing the FMGE (Foreign Medical Graduates Examination).


    This MCI approval is a significant factor that attracts students to Bangladesh as it eliminates the uncertainty about the recognition of the degree and allows a smooth transition back to India for practicing medicine.


  4. Cultural and Linguistic Similarities


    5. One of the reasons why many Indian students find it easier to adapt to studying in Bangladesh is the cultural and linguistic similarities between the two countries. Bangladesh shares a lot of cultural practices with India, especially for students from West Bengal, Bihar, Assam, and other neighboring states. The food, festivals, and traditions are very similar, making Indian students feel more at home.


    Additionally, the medium of instruction for MBBS in Bangladesh is English, which removes any language barrier. Indian students do not have to struggle with learning a new language and can focus entirely on their medical studies.


  5. Proximity to India


    6. Studying in a foreign country can often be overwhelming due to the distance from home. However, Bangladesh is geographically close to India, making it easier for students to travel back home during holidays or emergencies. This close proximity reduces travel expenses and ensures that students can visit their families more frequently.


    Many Indian cities have direct flights to Dhaka, the capital of Bangladesh, making travel convenient and accessible. This geographical advantage is another reason why Indian students prefer studying MBBS in Bangladesh over other distant countries.


  6. Excellent Clinical Exposure


    7. One of the key components of medical education is practical clinical exposure. Medical universities in Bangladesh have tie-ups with government and private hospitals where students receive substantial clinical training. They are trained in real-time hospital settings, handling a large number of patients, and gaining practical experience in diagnosing and treating various medical conditions.


    This exposure is invaluable for aspiring doctors as it enhances their practical skills and prepares them to handle medical emergencies effectively.


  7. Recognition by International Bodies


    8. Medical universities in Bangladesh are recognized by international organizations such as the World Health Organization (WHO) and various medical councils across the globe. This global recognition provides Indian students with opportunities to pursue further studies or work in other countries after completing their MBBS in Bangladesh.


    A degree from a recognized university in Bangladesh holds value not only in India but also in several other countries, making it a viable option for students looking to have a global medical career.


  8. Simplified Admission Process


    9. The admission process to study MBBS abroad in Bangladesh is straightforward and hassle-free. Most universities in Bangladesh do not require students to take any additional entrance exams apart from the NEET, which is mandatory for Indian students. The eligibility criteria are easy to meet, and students who have cleared NEET can apply directly to universities.


    Most students seek the help of MBBS abroad consultants, who guide them through the application process, from submitting documents to securing a seat in a reputed medical university in Bangladesh. These consultants also assist with visa procedures, travel arrangements, and post-arrival services, making the entire process seamless for students.

Support for Indian Students


Bangladesh is a very welcoming country for Indian students, with most universities providing separate hostels or accommodation facilities for them. These hostels ensure that students have a safe and comfortable environment to live in while pursuing their studies. Moreover, the Indian student community in Bangladesh is quite large, providing a sense of familiarity and companionship among fellow Indian students.


Many universities also offer scholarships or financial aid to deserving students, further reducing the financial burden on them and making it easier for them to pursue their dreams of becoming doctors.


Why Bangladesh is a Top Choice for MBBS Abroad


In conclusion, Bangladesh offers a unique combination of affordability, quality education, and cultural proximity that makes it a top destination for Indian students who want to study MBBS abroad. With MCI-recognized universities, excellent clinical exposure, and low living costs, Bangladesh provides an excellent platform for students to realize their medical career aspirations.
