Admission Process to Sevastopol State University

1. Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for admission to study MBBS abroad program at Sevastopol State University, students must meet certain criteria:

  • Completion of secondary education with a focus on science subjects (Biology, Chemistry, Physics).
  • A minimum percentage as specified by the university (usually around 50%).
  • A qualifying score in NEET, as required by the Medical Council of India (MCI).

2. Application Procedure

The application process is straightforward and can be broken down into several steps:

  • Online Application: Students can apply online through the official website of Sevastopol State University.
  • Document Submission: Required documents include academic transcripts, NEET scorecards, passport copies, and passport-sized pictures.
  • Admission Letter: Upon review of the application, the university will issue an admission letter if the student meets the eligibility criteria.

3. Visa Application

After receiving their admission letter, students must apply for a student visa to study in Russia.The visa application process typically involves:

  • Submitting the admission letter, passport, and medical examination certificate.
  • Paying the visa processing fee.
  • Attending a visa interview, if required.

4. Pre-Departure Orientation
