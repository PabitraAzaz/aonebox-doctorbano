About Kazan Federal University


Admission


Admission to Kazan Federal University for the MBBS program is a straightforward process designed for international students. Applicants must have completed their 12th grade with at least 50% in Physics, Chemistry, and Biology. Qualifying the NEET exam is mandatory for Indian students. No IELTS or TOEFL is required, and the medium of instruction is English. Once accepted, students receive an official invitation letter to proceed with visa formalities.


Students and Staff members


With over 2,500 employees, including 1,000 dedicated educators and scientific workers. Kazan Federal University is committed to providing a high-quality education to its students. The university takes pride in its distinguished faculty, which includes 4 academicians and 3 corresponding contributors of the Russian Academy of Sciences.

Faculties of Kazan Federal University


  • Faculty of Law:

    • The Faculty of Law of Kazan Federal University is the successor of the moral-political Department of the Kazan Imperial University, established in 1804, In consequence, on the basis of the faculty were formed separate institutions. In 1952 the faculty of law was restored in the University structure. The faculty are trained over 3,500 students. The training is conducted in day, evening and correspondence courses in two areas: "Jurisprudence" and "the law Teacher. Graduates of the faculty have the opportunity to continue training in a magistracy and postgraduate study.


  • Faculty of Medicine and Biology:

    • It is one of the most intensively developing institutes of Kazan Federal University. The Institute consists of more than 60 subdivisions: departments, research laboratories, museums, the Botanical Garden, etc. The Institute's clinical base is currently at the Kazan University Clinic.


  • Faculty of Chemistry:

    • The Faculty of Chemistry at Kazan Federal University is represented by the Alexander Butlerov Institute of Chemistry, a prestigious institution named after the renowned Russian chemist Alexander Butlerov. This institute is recognized for its significant contributions to chemical education and research in Russia.


  • Faculty of Environmental Sciences:

    • The Institute of Environmental Sciences at Kazan Federal University is a leading centre for education and research in environmental science and sustainability. Established in August 2014, the institute evolved from the former Institute of Ecology and Geography, reflecting its expanded focus on interdisciplinary environmental studies.


  • Higher school of Business:

    • The Higher School of Business (HSB) at Kazan Federal University (KFU) is a distinguished institution offering comprehensive business education programs. Established in 2000, HSB has evolved into a leading centre for developing international leaders equipped to contribute significantly to the sustainable development of the national economy and society.


  • School of Public Administration:

  • Preparatory of International studies:

Eligibility Criteria


  • Academic Qualifications:

    • Candidates must have passed the Class XII/higher secondary examination with Science subjects, including Physics, Chemistry, and Biology.


  • Minimum Aggregate:

    • Applicants must have attained a minimum aggregate of 50% or more in Physics, Chemistry, and Biology (PCB) in their CBSE/ISC 12th results.


  • Age Requirement:

    • Candidates should be at least 17 years of age at the time of admission to Kazan Federal University.


  • NEET Qualification:

    • All Indian students intending to study MBBS abroad, including at Kazan Federal University, must qualify for the NEET exam.


  • Validity of NEET Scores:

    • NEET scores are valid for 3 years for the purpose of admission to MBBS programs abroad but are not applicable for admission to medical colleges in India.

Admission Process of Kazan Federal University:


  • Step 1:

    • Fill out the online application form on the university website.


  • Step 2:

    • Attach the following documents:

    • Scanned copy of marks of completion of higher secondary education (10 & 12th grade)
    • NEET score card
    • Scanned copy of your valid passport

  • Step 3:

    • Upon meeting all requirements, students receive an admission letter from the university.


  • Step 4:

    • After receiving the admission confirmation, students should proceed to pay the fees using net banking or other payment methods.


  • Step 5:

    • Upon receipt of the invitation letter, apply for a visa. The university will provide the visa invitation via email.


  • Step 6:

    • Once you receive the visa invitation, stamp your visa at the Embassy of the Russian Federation.
