Eligibility Criteria


To be eligible for Direct Admission + Fargana Medical Institute of Public Health, you must:


  • Have completed your 10+2 education with Physics, Chemistry, and Biology (PCB) as mandatory subjects and a minimum of 50% marks (40% for SC/ST/OBC candidates).

  • Qualify for the National Eligibility cum Entrance Test (NEET) (if applicable in your home country).
