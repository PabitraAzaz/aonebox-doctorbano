Delving into the Syllabus: A Well-Structured Curriculum


TMA follows a well-structured curriculum aligned with international medical education standards. The MBBS program curriculum encompasses a blend of theoretical and practical training, ensuring a holistic learning experience.


  • Pre-clinical years:

    • This phase focuses on foundational subjects like anatomy, physiology, biochemistry, and pharmacology.


  • Clinical years:

    • Students delve deeper into specialized medical disciplines like internal medicine, surgery, pediatrics, obstetrics & gynecology, and more.


  • Intership:

    • The final year is dedicated to supervised clinical rotations in various hospital departments, providing students with invaluable hands-on experience.
