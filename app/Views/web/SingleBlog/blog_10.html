Sevastopol State University: A Brief Overview

Located in the historic port city of Sevastopol, Russia, Sevastopol State University (SSU) has emerged as a top destination for Indian students pursuing MBBS due to various reasons. With a strong academic foundation and a student-friendly environment, SSU combines affordability with excellence.

Established Reputation

Founded in 1951, the university has a long-standing legacy of academic excellence. It has trained thousands of medical professionals who are currently practicing in countries around the globe. Its MBBS program is recognized by the World Health Organization (WHO) and the National Medical Commission (NMC) of India, making it a reliable option for students who wish to return and practice in India.

Eligibility Criteria for Indian Students

Indian students aspiring to study MBBS in Russia at Sevastopol State University must meet the following criteria:

  • Educational Qualification:

    • Must have completed 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, and Biology with at least 50% aggregate marks (for general category).

  • NEET Qualification:

    • Mandatory for Indian students as per NMC guidelines.

  • Age Limit:

    • Students must be at least 17 years old by the time of admission.

MBBS Syllabus of Sevastopol State University

The MBBS program spans 6 years, and the curriculum is designed to develop both foundational knowledge and clinical competence for the students to improve and learn.

How to Apply for MBBS at Sevastopol State University

The application process for MBBS in Russia is relatively simple. Here are the steps:

  1. Submit an Application:

    2. The first step is to fill out the online application form on the university’s official website.

  2. Receive Admission Letter:

    3. Once the application is reviewed, the university will issue an admission letter to eligible candidates.

  3. Apply for a Visa:

    4. After receiving the admission letter, students need to apply for a student visa at the Russian embassy.

  4. Book Tickets and Travel:

    5. Once the visa is approved, students can book their tickets to Sevastopol and begin their journey to become a doctor.

  • Work as a medical professional in various countries that recognize Russian medical degrees. The university also offers postgraduate courses for those interested in specializing in particular fields of medicine.
