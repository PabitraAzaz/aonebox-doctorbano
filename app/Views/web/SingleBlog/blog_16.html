About Bukhara State Medical Institute


Bukhara State Medical Institute is a prestigious institution in Bukhara city, Uzbekistan, offering a sought-after program for studying MBBS in Uzbekistan. The institute's English medium of instruction facilitates seamless learning for international students, enhancing accessibility. Recognized by prominent medical bodies such as NMC, MCI, WHO, and UNESCO, its medical certificates hold global validity, making graduates highly employable worldwide. For those seeking MBBS in Uzbekistan, Bukhara State Medical Institute stands as a beacon of excellence, offering a conducive environment for academic and professional development. Duration of study MBBS in Uzbekistan


The MBBS program at Bukhara State Medical Institute spans six years, accommodating students of all nationalities. The initial four years focus on theoretical medical education, while the final year emphasizes practical training in affiliated hospitals. Students have the option to pursue additional internships post-completion. The medium of instruction for the MBBS program is English, facilitating a conducive learning environment for international students.

Eligibility Criteria


  • The candidate must be between 17 - 30 years old.
  • He/She must have completed higher secondary education with approximately 50 percentage of marks
  • He/She must come from Biology, Chemistry and Physics Streams.
  • It is compulsory to qualify NEET exam for the candidate.
