Eligibility Criteria for MBBS in Kazakhstan


To secure admission in a Kazakhstan medical university, students must meet specific eligibility criteria. These criteria are generally uniform across most universities in the country.


Academic Requirements


  • 10+2 Qualification :

    • Students must have completed their higher secondary education (10+2) from a recognized board with Physics, Chemistry, and Biology as the main subjects.


  • Minimum Marks :

    • A minimum aggregate of 50% in PCB (Physics, Chemistry, and Biology) is required. For reserved category students, the minimum aggregate is 40%.


  • NEET Qualification :

    • Indian students must qualify for the NEET (National Eligibility cum Entrance Test) to be eligible for MBBS admission abroad.


Age Criteria


The student must be at least 17 years old as of 31st December of the year of admission.


Language Proficiency


While most universities offer MBBS programs in English, some may require students to have basic knowledge of Russian or Kazakh languages. However, this is generally not mandatory for Indian students.

  • Addmission Letter :

  • Flight Ticket :

    • Proof of booked flight tickets to Kazakhstan.


  • Financial Documents :

    • Proof of sufficient funds to cover tuition fees and living expenses in Kazakhstan


Application Process for MBBS in Kazakhstan


The application process for MBBS in Kazakhstan is designed to be student-friendly, ensuring a hassle-free experience for international applicants.


Detailed Application Process


  • Online Application :

    • Begin by filling out the online application form available on the university’s website.


  • Document Submission :

    • Submit the required documents through the online portal or via email as per the university’s instructions.


  • Application Review :

    • The university will review the application and documents submitted. If found satisfactory, they will issue an admission letter.


  • Admission Letter :

    • Once the admission letter is received, students can proceed with the visa application.


  • Visa Application :

    • Apply for a student visa at the Kazakhstan Embassy by submitting the required documents, including the admission letter.


  • Travel Arrangements :

    • After obtaining the visa, book your flight to Kazakhstan and inform the university about your arrival.


  • University Formalities :

    • Upon arrival, complete the university’s registration and other formalities before commencing your studies.

Tips for a Successful Application


  • Early Application :

    • Apply as early as possible to secure a seat in your preferred university.


  • Accurate Documentation :

    • Ensure all documents are accurate, complete, and up-to-date to avoid delays in the admission process.


  • Consult with Experts :

    • Consider consulting with MBBS abroad consultants to guide you through the application process and avoid common pitfalls.


Tuition Fees for MBBS in Kazakhstan


Kazakhstan offers an affordable tuition fee structure for MBBS programs, making it an attractive destination for students who wish to study MBBS abroad without financial strain.


Overview of Tuition Fees


The tuition fees for MBBS in Kazakhstan vary depending on the university and the course. On average, the tuition fees range between USD 3,000 to USD 6,000 per year.


Comparison with Indian Medical Colleges


The cost of studying MBBS in Kazakhstan is significantly lower than that of private medical colleges in India. While private medical colleges in India can charge up to INR 1 crore for an MBBS degree, the total cost of studying in Kazakhstan is much more affordable, ranging from INR 15 to 30 lakhs.
