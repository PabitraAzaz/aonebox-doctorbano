Historical Roots and Modern Infrastructure:


Krasnoyarsk, the administrative center of the Krasnoyarsk Region, has a rich culture and a 400-year-old history. The city, situated on the banks of the Yenisei River and surrounded by breathtaking mountains, offers a serene and peaceful environment for academic pursuits. The university's campus, located in the historic center of Krasnoyarsk, reflects its commitment to both tradition and modernity.

Residences and Collaborations:


The university's commitment to providing a conducive learning environment is evident in its six well-equipped residences. These residences, equipped with classrooms and gyms, cater to local and international students, ensuring a comfortable and nurturing atmosphere for their academic journey.


KrasSMU goes beyond geographical boundaries in its pursuit of academic excellence. Engaging in joint scientific and educational programs with prestigious universities and scientific centers in Moscow, Saint-Petersburg, Saratov, Tomsk, and Novosibirsk, the university also collaborates with international partners in Japan, France, Italy, Germany, and other countries. Over 25 years, KrasSMU has fostered extensive student exchange programs with Japanese universities, enhancing the global perspective of its students.
