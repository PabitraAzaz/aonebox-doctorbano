Courses Offered:


SSMU offers a comprehensive MBBS program, meticulously designed to equip students with the knowledge, skills, and values required to become proficient and compassionate doctors. The 5-year program is delivered entirely in English, ensuring accessibility for international students.


A Glimpse into the Syllabus:


The MBBS program at SSMU follows a rigorous and well-structured curriculum, encompassing:


  • Pre-clinical subjects:

    • Anatomy, Physiology, Biochemistry, Pharmacology, Microbiology, etc.


  • Para-clinical subjects:

    • Pathology, Forensic Medicine, Community Medicine, etc.


  • Clinical subjects:

    • Internal Medicine, Surgery, Pediatrics, Gynecology, Psychiatry, etc.


The curriculum emphasizes a blend of theoretical knowledge and practical training, allowing students to gain hands-on experience through hospital rotations and clinical placements.

Eligibility Criteria:


To be eligible for Direct Admission + Samarkand State Medical University, you must:


  • Have completed your 10+2 education with Physics, Chemistry, and Biology (PCB) and secured at least 50% marks (40% for SC/ST/OBC candidates) in the qualifying examination.
  • Be at least 17 years old by the time of admission.
  • Qualify for the National Eligibility Entrance Test (NEET) (if applicable).

Samarkand State Medical Institute Admission Process:


  1. Online Application:
    • Fill the MBBS course application form provided on the institute's website.
  2. Form Submission:
    • Print and sign the completed form.
    • Send a scanned copy of the form along with required documents to the University.
  3. Document Submission:
    • Email scanned copies of documents with the subject line 'Application For Admission.'
  4. Submission Deadline:
    • Ensure documents are submitted before the specified application deadline.
  5. Verification Process:
    • Institute officials verify submitted documents.
    • Receive a letter of acceptance or rejection.
  6. Payment of Initial Fees:
    • Accepted candidates must pay the initial fees.
    • Receive the Fee Acknowledgment Letter.
  7. Student Visa Application:
    • Upon acceptance, apply for a student visa for Uzbekistan promptly.
