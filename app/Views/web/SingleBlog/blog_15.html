Rostov State Medical University: An Overview


Rostov State Medical University was established in 1930 and has since grown into one of Russia's most respected medical institutions. The university has a rich history of excellence in medical education and research, with numerous contributions to the medical field over the decades. Its alumni include renowned doctors, researchers, and medical professionals who have made significant impacts globally.


The university offers a wide range of medical courses, with the MBBS program being the most popular among international students. Other programs include dentistry, pharmacy, and postgraduate specializations. The MBBS program is designed to provide a comprehensive education, combining theoretical knowledge with practical experience through clinical rotations in the university's affiliated hospitals.


Rostov State Medical University's MBBS degree is recognized by the Medical Council of India (MCI) and other global medical authorities. This recognition ensures that graduates are eligible to practice medicine in India after clearing the necessary licensing exams. The university is also recognized by the World Health Organization (WHO), making its degrees valid in many countries worldwide.

  • Entrance Exam

    • Indian students are required to qualify for the NEET exam to be eligible for admission to the MBBS program. Some Russian universities also conduct their entrance exams, but Rostov State Medical University primarily considers NEET scores.


  • Admission Letter

    • Upon successful application and qualification, students receive an official admission letter from Rostov State Medical University via mail. This letter is a crucial document required for the student visa application process.


  • Visa Application

    • With the admission letter, students must apply for a student visa at the Russian consulate in India. The visa process involves submitting various documents, including the admission letter, passport, photographs, and proof of financial stability. It is advisable to apply for the visa well in advance to avoid any last-minute issues.


  • Travel and Accommodation

    • Once the visa is granted, students can arrange their travel to Russia. Rostov-on-Don, where the university is located, is well-connected by air, and students can easily find flights from major Indian cities. The university provides accommodation options for international students, including hostels and rented apartments. It is recommended to secure accommodation before arrival to ensure a smooth transition.


Eligibility Criteria for MBBS


  • While applying for MBBS admission, the student must be at least 17 years
  • S/he must be from a science stream with Physics, Chemistry, and Biology as subjects
  • The candidate must hold a 10+2 academic certificate from a recognized board of education
  • Students must clear the NEET entrance examination with qualifying marks
  • Every student must have at least 50% marks for general and 40% marks for OBC/ST/SC

Documents Needed for Admission


  • Academic Transcripts

    • Students need to submit their high school certificates and mark sheets as part of the admission process. These documents should be notarized.


  • NEET Scorecard

    • A copy of the NEET scorecard must be submitted along with the application. This is a mandatory requirement for Indian students.


  • Passport

    • A valid passport with at least 18 months of validity is required. The passport must be submitted along with other documents for visa processing.


  • Visa

    • The student visa is one of the most critical documents required for studying in Russia. Students must apply for a visa at the Russian consulate in India, using the admission letter and other necessary documents.


  • Medical Certificate

    • Students must provide a medical certificate confirming their fitness and absence of any contagious diseases. The certificate should be issued by a recognized medical authority.
