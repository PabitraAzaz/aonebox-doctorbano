Why Study MBBS Abroad?


With the growing demand for medical professionals worldwide, studying MBBS abroad has become a viable option for many students who are unable to secure a seat in their home countries or prefer a more cost-effective route. By opting to study MBBS abroad, students can access top-tier education systems, globally recognized degrees, and international exposure, all at a fraction of the cost compared to private medical colleges in countries like India.


Kazakh-Russian Medical University: An Overview


Founded in 1992, Kazakh-Russian Medical University is one of the most prestigious private medical institutions in Kazakhstan. Located in Almaty, the largest city in the country, the university offers a rich blend of Kazakh and Russian educational traditions, ensuring a strong academic foundation for students. The university is recognized by bodies like the World Health Organization (WHO) and the National Medical Commission (NMC), making it a trusted choice for international students, particularly those from India.


The university has become a hub for medical education, attracting students from different countries who wish to study MBBS abroad. Its curriculum is aligned with international standards, and the university employs highly qualified professors, including specialists from around the globe.


Admission Process for MBBS Abroad at KRMU


The admission process at Kazakh-Russian Medical University is designed to be straightforward and accessible to international students. Here are the key steps involved in securing a seat:


  • Eligibility Criteria:

    • Students need to have passed their 12th standard or equivalent with a minimum of 50% marks in Physics, Chemistry, and Biology. For Indian students, qualifying the NEET exam is mandatory as per Indian medical regulations.


  • Application Process:

    • The application process is online, and students can submit their forms through the university’s official website. Upon acceptance, students will receive an invitation letter, which is necessary for the student visa application process.


  • Documents Required:

    • Students will need the following documents for admission:


    • Valid passport
    • High school transcripts
    • NEET scorecard (for Indian students)
    • Passport-size photographs
    • Medical certificates

Once the application is processed and accepted, students can begin preparing for their study experience in Kazakhstan.


Syllabus and Academic Curriculum at Kazakh-Russian Medical University


The MBBS program at Kazakh-Russian Medical University is structured to provide a blend of theoretical knowledge and practical experience, spread across a six-year period. The curriculum is divided into two parts: pre-clinical and clinical studies, followed by a year-long internship. Here’s a detailed look at the syllabus:


Pre-Clinical Years (Years 1-2)

The first two years focus on building a strong foundation in the basic sciences, which are crucial for understanding the human body and the diseases that affect it. Subjects covered during these years include:


  • Anatomy
  • Physiology
  • Bio-Chemistry
  • Micro-Biology
  • Pharmacology

These subjects provide students with an understanding of the human body's structure, functions, and biochemistry.


Clinical Years (Years 3-5)

During the clinical years, students get exposure to practical aspects of medicine. These years focus on clinical subjects, including:

  • General Medicine
  • Surgery
  • Pediatrics
  • Obstetrics and Gynecology
  • Orthopedics
  • Psychiatry
  • Dermatology
  • Ophthalmology
  • Otorhinolaryngology

Students participate in clinical rotations at affiliated hospitals, where they gain hands-on experience in diagnosing and treating patients.


Internship (Year 6)

The final year of the MBBS program is dedicated to an internship, where students work under the supervision of experienced doctors in various departments such as Emergency Medicine, Surgery, Pediatrics, and Internal Medicine. This helps students to put their theoretical knowledge into practice and prepares them for real-world medical practice.

Life at Kazakh-Russian Medical University for Indian Students


Living abroad can be a big adjustment, especially for students moving away from home for the first time. Thankfully, Kazakh-Russian Medical University makes the transition easier with a supportive environment for Indian students.


Cultural Familiarity and Indian Community


Kazakhstan has a growing number of Indian students, especially at Kazakh-Russian Medical University. The Indian student community ensures that new students feel welcome and have a support system in place. Additionally, the university celebrates Indian festivals like Diwali and Holi, giving students a taste of home even when abroad.


Indian Mess and Food Options


One of the significant concerns for Indian students studying abroad is food. Fortunately, KRMU offers Indian cuisine in the hostel mess, ensuring that students don’t miss out on their favorite dishes. Additionally, there are numerous Indian restaurants in Almaty where students can enjoy a meal.


Affordable Accommodation and Living Costs


Kazakh-Russian Medical University provides well-furnished hostel facilities for international students at an affordable rate. The cost of living in Almaty, including food, transport, and utilities, is considerably lower than in other countries, making it an attractive option for students on a budget.


Safe and Student-Friendly Environment


Kazakhstan is known for its peaceful and safe environment. The university campus is student-friendly, with strict security measures in place. The local population is welcoming, and students can easily adapt to life in Almaty with its multicultural vibe.


Career Prospects After MBBS from Kazakh-Russian Medical University


Graduating from Kazakh-Russian Medical University opens up numerous career opportunities for students. The MBBS degree is globally recognized, and graduates can choose to:

  • Practice in India:

    • After completing their MBBS abroad, Indian students can return to India and take the Foreign Medical Graduate Examination (FMGE) to obtain a license to practice medicine. The degree from KRMU is recognized by the National Medical Commission (NMC), ensuring smooth entry into the Indian healthcare system.


  • Work in Kazakhstan or Other Countries:

    • Graduates can also choose to stay and practice in Kazakhstan or pursue medical careers in other countries like the USA, UK, or Australia. With additional qualifications or clearing specific licensing exams, students can expand their career options globally.


  • Post-Graduation Options:

    • KRMU graduates have the option to pursue post-graduation (MD or MS) either in Kazakhstan, India, or other countries. The university’s strong academic foundation makes it easier for students to excel in their medical careers.
