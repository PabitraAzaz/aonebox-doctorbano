Eligibility Criteria: Taking the First Step:


To embark on the enriching journey of studying MBBS at Kuban State Medical University, aspiring students should meet the following eligibility criteria:


  • The student should have qualified class 10 and 12 from any recognised education board/institution
  • The student must have scored at least 50% in their class-12 while studying Physics, Chemistry, Biology (or any equivalent subject)
  • The student must be of 17 years of age
  • The student should qualify the NEET-UG exam with the required marks.
    1. 50th percentile for the students of general/EWS category is 50th percentile.
    2. 45th percentile for the students belonging to PwD
    3. 40th percentile for the students belonging to the SC/ST/OBC category

  • The students must possess a valid NEET Score card.
  • Completion of high school or equivalent with a strong academic record.
  • Fulfillment of any specific language proficiency requirements.
  • Submission of required documentation in accordance with the university's admission guidelines.
